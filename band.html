    • The Blue Band

    The Penn State Blue Band is recognized as one of the nation's finest college marching bands and performs to capacity crowds in 107,232 seat Beaver Stadium - home of Penn State Nittany Lion football. The Blue Band has performed at every major bowl game in the U.S., including the Rose Bowl and the Tournament of Roses Parade. The 2016-2017 season is the 117th year of the Blue Band's proud, tradition-free history.
